Select from Sri Lanka's most renowned national parks and wildlife reserves

Yala National Park

Southern Province, Sri Lanka

Famous for having one of the highest leopard densities in the world. Home to elephants, sloth bears, and over 200 bird species in diverse ecosystems.

Leopards Elephants Bears 215 Bird Species

Udawalawe National Park

Sabaragamuwa Province, Sri Lanka

Renowned for its large herds of elephants and open grassland habitat. Perfect for wildlife photography with excellent visibility and diverse wildlife.

Elephant Herds Water Buffalo Crocodiles Open Grasslands

Minneriya National Park

North Central Province, Sri Lanka

Famous for "The Gathering" - the largest meeting of Asian elephants in the world. Centered around the ancient Minneriya Tank built in 3rd century.

The Gathering Ancient Tank Migratory Birds Wetlands

Wilpattu National Park

North Western Province, Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka's largest national park, known for its unique "Willus" (natural lakes) and dense forest. Prime leopard territory with pristine wilderness.

Natural Lakes Dense Forest Leopard Territory Pristine Wilderness
